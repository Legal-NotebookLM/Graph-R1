**C.** The Respondent shall pay interest @ 19% per annum on the said amount from 26th November 2024 till actual payment/realization.

**D.** The Respondent shall pay the following costs:
   - Arbitrator's fees: ‚Çπ60,000/-

   - Administrative expenses: ‚Çπ15,000/-
   - **Total Costs: ‚Çπ1,15,000/-**

**F.** If the Respondent fails to make payment within 30 days, the Claimant shall be at liberty to enforce this Award in accordance with law.

**G.** In the event of non-payment, the Claimant may attach and sell any assets of the Respondent including salon equipment (if traceable) for adjustment of dues.

**H.** This Award shall be binding on both parties.
